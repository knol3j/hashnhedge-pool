‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a GPU to mine?

No! HashNHedge mining works with any CPU. While GPUs can mine faster, they're not required.

How much can I earn?

You earn 1 HNH token per valid share. Earnings depend on your hashrate and luck. Average CPU miners earn 50-200 HNH per day.

When do I get paid?

Rewards are instant! HNH tokens are sent to your wallet as soon as you submit a valid share.

Is this safe for my computer?

Yes! The miner uses only spare CPU cycles and won't damage your hardware. You can adjust the intensity in settings.
